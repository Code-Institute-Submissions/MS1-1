ABOUT

Shivananda yoga studio is much more than just yoga classes. Every event, every connection, every stretch transpires from the founding desire to make yoga inclusive, accessible and empowering for everyone. We strive to promote positive body image and connectedness within the community. And above all, we believe everyone can benefit from the power of connection that yoga teaches.

We offer many varieties of Yoga, including Hatha, Ashtanga, Iyengar, Vinyasa, Mindfulness, Meditation, Kundalini and many other variations dedicated to improve fitness, flexibility, physical, emotional and overall wellbeing. Classes are both challenging and creative as well as enjoyable and inspiring. Classes are provided by various teachers who are not only experienced but also patient, dedicated and willing to help and guide you through your yoga journey.
