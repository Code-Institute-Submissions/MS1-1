ABOUT

Shivananda yoga studio is much more than just yoga classes. Every event, every connection, every stretch transpires from the founding desire to make yoga inclusive, accessible and empowering for everyone. We strive to promote positive body image and connectedness within the community. And above all, we believe everyone can benefit from the power of connection that yoga teaches.

CLASSES & TIMINGS

****Classes run 7 days a week****

TIMES CLASS DESCRIPTION
6 - 9 AM Hatha Yoga physical stretching and breathing suitable for all
4 - 6 PM Iyengar Yoga focus on alignment as well as detailed and precise movements. Intermediate and Advanced only.
6 - 9 pm Kundalini Yoga Kundalini yoga practice is equal parts spiritual and physical. Suitable for all.

OUR TEACHERS

Jane Row

Jane Row combines her degrees in counseling and psychology with her yoga certification to assist her clients in meeting their personal and professional goals and improving overall well-being.

Neera Nuff

Neera’s classes offer variations to make yoga accessible to everyone, no matter experience, size, age, or ability. Neera’s passion is taking yoga into our communities as a tool for healing.

Anastasia Hausen

Anastasia teaching style is approachable but challenging, with good music. Her classes are mostly fast paced with very long and winding sequences designed to get you out of your head.
